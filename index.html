SortEm

An Advanced Email Summarization and Sorting System Using Transformer Based NLP Techniques

2024 - 2025

In the era of digital communication, email overload has become a significant challenge for professionals and students alike. This presents SortEm, an advanced email management system designed to streamline email communications through automated summarization and categorization. By leveraging state-of-the-art Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques, including transformer-based models like BART and zero-shot learning, SortEm provides concise email summaries and intelligent categorization, enabling users to focus on critical communications. The system also incorporates a Text-to-Speech (TTS) module, enhancing accessibility for users with visual impairments or those who prefer auditory content.




QCut

Skip The Queue, Enjoy The Food

2024

Qcut is a restaurant management system that allows users to easily place orders and track their progress. The system is designed to be user-friendly and intuitive, making it easy to navigate and place orders. It solves the problem of queues by allowing users to place orders online and track their progress in real-time.
